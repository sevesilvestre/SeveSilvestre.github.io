About Me

In 2018, I started my journey at Chapman University as a freshman majoring in Health Sciences, hoping to become an orthodontist and join my older brother in running a family-owned practice. However, after four years of self-reflection and exploration, I graduated with a degree in Business Marketing and a minor in Data Analytics. Growing up, I had always loved math, and the moment I touched Python in my first Computer Science course, I knew that working with data was my true calling.

Today, I am a Data Analyst at Hyundai Motor America, working in the Customer Affairs department. I use my knowledge of data wrangling, manipulation, and visualization to provide data-driven insights into business metrics, helping to identify and solve critical challenges related to customer issues.

Outside of data, I enjoy reading self-improvement books to encourage myself to improve 1% every day (shoutout "Atomic Habits" by James Clear!), staying active by going to the gym or playing tennis, and exploring my passion for content creation and video production. I'm always eager to learn new things and share my knowledge with others. If you're interested in connecting, so feel encouraged to reach out to me and connect with me on my socials!
